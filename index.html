Common Pests in Michigan

Michigan is home to a variety of pests that can affect homes and businesses. Understanding these pests is the first step in effective management and prevention.


5 Most Popular Pests in Michigan

  • Ants
  • Mosquitoes
  • Carpenter Ants
  • Rodents
  • Spiders

Seasonal enemies calendar

Different pests are more prevalent during certain seasons. For example, mosquitoes are more common in the summer, while rodents may seek shelter indoors during the colder months.
For current winter season, the most common pests in Michigan include:

  • Boxelder bugs
  • Cluster flies
  • Asian lady beetles
  • Brown marmorated stink bugs
